Key Findings

Students discover HPU through trusted relationships but choose HPU because it aligns their physical comfort, professional ambition, and personal values into one seamless collegiate experience.

Section 1-How Awareness Begins

Discovery at HPU is driven by trusted personal recommendations and the universitys reputation for supporting the whole student. OARS bridges awareness to enrollment by removing barriers, ensuring equal access, and fostering self-advocacy and independence in alignment with HPUs Premier Life Skills philosophy.

Trusted Voices to Personal Commitment

Section I revealed that the path to High Point University is paved by trusted voices. Whether through a family friend, a social media influencer, or a high school counselor, discovery is rooted in external validation. However, as we move into Section II, we see a transformation: the student moves from listening to others to experiencing the institution for themselves, shifting from a single source of awareness to a multi-layered 'Whole Person' commitment.

Insight

At High Point University, enrollment decisions are driven by a holistic experience rather than a single factor. About 75.7% of students choose HPU for the combination of its supportive environment, Premier Life Skills focus, and strong sense of belonging, creating a safe, enriching community that feels like home.

The HPU Advantage

The "magic" of HPU's enrollment is that students do not have to make trade-offs. They can have a beautiful campus and strong professional preparation, and pursue their chosen major within a values-based community. By supporting the whole personthe body through comfort and safety, the mind through academics and life skills, and the heart through values and belongingHigh Point University offers a complete experience that students find hard to pass up.
